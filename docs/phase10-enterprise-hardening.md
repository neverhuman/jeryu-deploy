# Phase 10 Enterprise Hardening

Phase 10 hardens Jeryu for enterprise operation with:

- SSO validation for OIDC and SAML.
- Tenant-scoped RBAC with explainable decisions.
- Multi-tenant boundary checks.
- Append-only audit receipts.
- Backup and restore drill invariants.
- Upgrade and rollback plans.
- Red-team gates for tenant isolation, SSO misconfiguration, audit tampering, and rollback safety.

All security-sensitive behavior fails closed.
